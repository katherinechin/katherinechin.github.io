Hi! My name is Katie, and I am a nerd who likes to code up my website! In one week, I learned HTML + CSS and how to configure this layout, all from scratch.

In 2018, I graduated from business school Bentley University, majoring in global management and media arts. Currently, I found (and am pursuing!) my passion for coding when Processing piqued my curiosity and provided me the tools to explore my creativity.

I was a competitive figure skater for the Skating Club of Boston, trained under Konstantin Kostin for eight years before injury. Additionally, I was on the Ellyn Robinson Weightlifting team, qualifying for US junior nationals in my first year of training. In recent years, I had participated in Harvard ballroom dancing as well as Boston University parkour.

In recent years, I lived two years abroad in total, mainly in Hong Kong (HKU), Australia (Bond University), United Kingdom, and Switzerland.

I enjoy international photography, traveling on my penny board, sometimes both together!
