Moves in the Field App


As a singles figure skater, there are eight levels of moves to pass that consists of 40+ moves in total. The diagrams of these moves are in a 500+ page rulebook that includes other content not related to the Singles discipline, such as ice dancing moves and rules for judges. I made an app to concisely focus on providing these singles Moves in Field diagrams on a mobile user-friendly interface for skaters and coaches.

This app focuses on putting all diagrams in a an abridged, organized format so skaters may conveniently access the redrawn diagrams on their phone rather than having to carry and purchase bulky rulebooks.
